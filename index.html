ABOUT US

Empowering and developing the youth community through Basketball

Mission Statement

West Coast Basketball Association mission is to provide an environment where young and upcoming athletes can have the opportunity to learn and develop the fundamental skills of basketball. A recreational-based program designed to encourage developmental growth in a fun and competitive setting.

Vision

Our Vision is to provide a non-judgemental environement where athletes feel welcomed, feel a sense of belonging; a sense of community. WCBBA provides ALL athletes with the opportunity to participate, compete, and most importantly, have fun.

Youth Development

Healthy competitive sports is important, however, our ultimate goal is to cultivate learning. We want children and adolescents to develop values that can be used outside the basketball courts; in real life situations. Team sports allows kids to become goal oriented, recognize accountability, to pesevere through losses and mistakes, be a team player, build character and understand hard work. These are skills that become life lessons for our youth community.
